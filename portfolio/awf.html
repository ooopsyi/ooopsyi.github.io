Conservation Canines

This is a responsive “yearbook” landing page that introduces AWF’s first graduating class of sniffer dogs and educates the user about what they do in the field. After clicking through some adorable dog imagery and brief information, users can leave their message of support for the canines on the page.



This campaign resulted in thousands of positive messages on both the landing page and through social media, and brought on new donors who were just learning about the effectiveness of anti-poaching canines. See the site >
