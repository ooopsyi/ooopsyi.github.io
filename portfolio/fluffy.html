Fluffy Cloud Lamp

Fluffy is a programmable portable lamp designed by Yi Rui and Eliza Tan as a side project. By combining Attiny85 chip, Led strip and the Arduino IDE, we aim to launch this product as an educational kit for children to learn basic programming and handcraft.
